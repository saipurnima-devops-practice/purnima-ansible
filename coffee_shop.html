Our story

We started with one obsession

In 2015, our founder Meera Krishnan left a career in architecture to pursue a simpler brief: build a room where one cup of coffee could be extraordinary. Brewed & Bound has been that room ever since.

We source directly from farms in Coorg, Chikmagalur, and Araku Valley — and travel there every harvest season. We roast in-house, weekly, in small batches.
